About MARS

The Mars Rover Society at IIT Roorkee (MARS IITR) is a student team dedicated to designing and building the next generation of Mars rovers. Our mission is to push the boundaries of space exploration technology while fostering innovation and technical excellence among students.

Founded in 2018, we participate in prestigious international competitions like the University Rover Challenge (URC) and the Indian Rover Challenge (IRC), where we consistently rank among the top teams globally.
